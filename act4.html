Lore

Corrupted paladins of an ancient order, Doom Knights are brutal and relentless. Now servants of Diablo, they exist only to destroy.

Notable Drops

  • Normal: Great swords, mana potions
  • Nightmare: Rare plate armor, runes (Shael–Sol)
  • Hell: Unique swords, runes (Um–Gul)

Affixes

  • Prefix: Frenzied – Increases attack speed
  • Suffix: of Wrath – Adds bonus cold damage
