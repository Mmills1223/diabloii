Lore

Doom Knights are undead warriors forged in the flames of the Burning Hells. Once noble champions, they now serve the Prime Evils as relentless enforcers of chaos.

Notable Drops

  • Normal: War Swords, Gothic Shields, Runes (Ral–Tal)
  • Nightmare: Rare claymores, magic armor, runes (Ort–Sol)
  • Hell: Elite class swords, runes (Um–Gul), unique plate armor

Affixes

  • Prefix: Spectral – Increases resistances and phasing
  • Suffix: of Pain – Adds open wounds effect

Lore

Abyss Knights are corrupted archers of Hell, bound to spew dark energies in long-range volleys. Their elemental strength grows stronger the deeper into the abyss they reside.

  • Nightmare: Magic crossbows, runes (Tal–Shael), jewels
  • Hell: Unique bows, runes (Um–Ist), crafted gear

Lore

The youngest of the Prime Evils, Diablo is the embodiment of fear and terror. Imprisoned beneath Tristram and then unleashed, he now rules the Chaos Sanctuary in Act IV, awaiting challengers to his throne.

Notable Drops

  • Normal: Rare jewels, rings, and armor
  • Nightmare: Unique weapons, runes (Io–Um), high magic gear
  • Hell: Unique class-specific items, runes (Mal–Gul), elite armor and charms

Affixes

  • Prefix: Infernal – Fire enchanted burst
  • Suffix: of Terror – Fear aura drains stamina and defense
