Lore

These rotting corpses were once townsfolk, now reanimated by the corruption spreading through the land. They shuffle aimlessly, driven by an insatiable hunger for the living.

Notable Drops

  • Normal: Minor healing potions, daggers
  • Nightmare: Magic boots, runes (El–Ral)
  • Hell: Unique clubs, runes (Pul–Um)
